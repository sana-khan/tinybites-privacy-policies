

### 1. Who we are

Tiny Bites ("the app", "we", "us") is a baby-food companion published
by the Tiny Bites team. You can reach us at **greysonmiller67@gmail.com**
for any privacy question or request below.

### 2. What we collect, and what we don't

Tiny Bites is designed to collect **as little data as possible**.
Everything you enter — your baby's name, age stage, dietary notes,
flagged allergies, logged tastes, planned meals, saved recipes and
notification preferences — is stored **only on your device**, inside
the app's private storage area provided by iOS or Android.

We do **not**:

- create or require any account;
- ask for your email, phone number, address, or any contact detail;
- upload or sync your baby's profile or activity to any server;
- sell, rent or share your data with anyone for marketing purposes;
- use cookies, web beacons, fingerprinting, or session-replay tools;
- track your location.

When you tap **Settings → Export my data**, a JSON file is generated
on your device and handed to the system share sheet. What happens to
that file from there is up to you — Tiny Bites never sees or stores it.

When you tap **Settings → Delete all data**, your local storage is
wiped immediately and the app restarts in its first-install state.

### 3. Advertising (Google AdMob)

Tiny Bites is free. To support the costs of running it, the app
displays a small number of advertisements served by **Google AdMob**.

To deliver and measure ads, AdMob may read your device's
**advertising identifier** (the resettable "Advertising ID" on
Android; "Identifier for Advertisers" / IDFA on iOS, only with your
explicit App Tracking Transparency permission). The app requests
**non-personalised ads** by default, which limits the data Google
uses to choose the ad you see.

You can reset or limit your advertising identifier any time:

- **iOS** — *Settings → Privacy & Security → Tracking* and *Apple
  Advertising*.
- **Android** — *Settings → Google → Ads*.

Google's processing of advertising data is governed by Google's own
privacy policy: <https://policies.google.com/privacy>. We have no
visibility into individual ad requests beyond aggregate, anonymised
performance counts surfaced in the AdMob console.

### 4. Children and parental responsibility

Tiny Bites is intended for **adults caring for babies**, not for use
by children themselves. The information you enter is about a child
but is **entered by you**, the parent or carer, and is treated as
your personal data for the purposes of this policy.

Because no profile data leaves the device, no information about your
baby is ever transmitted, collected, or held by us. We do not
knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe
a child has somehow submitted personal information through Tiny
Bites, contact us at the email above and we will take the matter
seriously.

In particular, Tiny Bites is not directed at, and does not knowingly
operate on, the data of users under the age of 13 (United States,
COPPA) or under 16 (European Economic Area, GDPR Art. 8). Ad serving
through Google AdMob is configured to treat the app as **not
primarily child-directed** under Google's Families policies, but
ad personalisation is disabled regardless.

### 5. Your rights

Depending on where you live, you may have rights under laws including
the EU/UK General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), the California
Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA/CPRA), and similar regulations elsewhere
to:

- access the personal data we hold about you;
- correct inaccurate data;
- have your data erased;
- restrict or object to processing;
- export ("port") your data.

Because Tiny Bites stores your data only on your device, you can
exercise most of these rights yourself, instantly, from
**Settings → Data & storage**:

- **Where your data lives** — read what's stored.
- **Export my data** — produces a JSON file containing everything.
- **Delete all data** — wipes the local store.

For any request you cannot resolve in-app — or for any question about
how Google AdMob processes the advertising identifier — email
**feedback@tinybites.app** and we will respond within 30 days.

### 6. Security

Your data is stored inside Tiny Bites' app sandbox, protected by the
operating system's per-app file isolation. We rely on iOS/Android
device encryption, which is on by default on every modern phone. No
data leaves the device, so there is no transmission to secure.

### 7. Third-party services we use

- **Google AdMob** — advertising, see §3. Privacy policy:
  <https://policies.google.com/privacy>.
- **Expo / React Native** — the framework the app is built on. Expo
  does not collect end-user data from this app in the managed
  workflow we use.
- **Google Fonts (Quicksand, Nunito Sans)** — the font files are
  bundled with the app. Fonts are not fetched at runtime, so no
  network requests are made to Google for them after install.

We do not integrate any analytics SDK, crash reporter, attribution
network, push-notification provider, or social-login provider.
